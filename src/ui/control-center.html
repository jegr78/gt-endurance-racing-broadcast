The relay reads its schedule and HUD data from the Google Sheet and is configured through Settings: IRO_SHEET_ID (the sheet) and IRO_SHEET_PUSH_URL (write-back for the timer + panel). There are no other local relay settings — tab names, ports and the bind mode use safe defaults.
